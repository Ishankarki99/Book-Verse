Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about BookVerse.

Is BookVerse free to use?

Yes! BookVerse is completely free to browse, search, and save favorites. We believe everyone should have access to great book recommendations without barriers.

How do I save books to my favorites?

Simply click the heart icon on any book card or in the book details modal. Your favorites are saved locally in your browser using Local Storage, so they persist between visits.

Can I purchase books through BookVerse?

Currently, BookVerse is a discovery platform. We help you find great books, and you can purchase them through your preferred bookstore or online retailer. Purchase integration is on our roadmap.

How are book recommendations generated?

Our recommendations are curated by our editorial team based on trending titles, reader ratings, and community favorites. We're working on personalized AI-powered recommendations for the future.

How do I switch between dark and light mode?

Click the moon/sun icon in the navigation bar to toggle between dark and light themes. Your preference is saved automatically and will be remembered on your next visit.

How can I suggest a book to be added?

We love hearing from our community! Use the contact form above with the subject "Book Suggestion" and tell us about the title you'd like to see on BookVerse.
